Payroll Detail — Net to Gross
How each Net salary grosses up to full employer cost

To pay each employee the exact Net they were promised, the company grosses up the base salary to absorb the employee's statutory deductions — 11% social insurance (on wages up to the 16,700 EGP cap) plus Egypt's progressive income-tax brackets. On top of the gross, the employer pays its own 18.75% social-insurance share. The final column is the true monthly cost per role, which is what feeds the Operating Expenses above.
